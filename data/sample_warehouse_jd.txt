JOB TITLE: Warehouse Team Lead
COMPANY: LogiCorp Distribution Centre

ABOUT THE ROLE
We are hiring a Warehouse Team Lead to oversee daily operations
at our Bangalore distribution facility.

RESPONSIBILITIES
- Supervise a team of 10 warehouse associates
- Ensure safe operation of all material handling equipment
- Manage inventory accuracy using our WMS system
- Conduct daily cycle counts and stock reconciliation
- Enforce OSHA safety standards across all shifts
- Coordinate inbound receiving and outbound shipping

REQUIRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Experience with forklift and pallet jack operation
- Knowledge of warehouse receiving and shipping processes
- Familiarity with inventory management procedures
- Understanding of workplace safety and OSHA compliance
- Basic team supervision experience

PREFERRED QUALIFICATIONS
- Experience with Warehouse Management Systems
- Six Sigma or quality management knowledge
- Supply chain fundamentals
